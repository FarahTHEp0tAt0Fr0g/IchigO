AGATSUMA ZENITSU DELUXE EDITION 1/4 SCALE STATUE

PRODUCT DESCRIPTION

From Infinity Studios, introducing the Deluxe Edition 1/4 scale statue of Agatsuma Zanitsu.


This statue depicts Zenitsu Agatsuma unleashing Thunder Breathing First Form: Thunderclap and Flash in the Natagumo Mountain Arc. Heavily injured in combat, he directs all of his power to his feet, sprints onward, and strikes at their adversary with great speed and strength. Zenitsu's eyes are illuminated by lightning. His electric radiance erupts, causing the concrete underneath him to shatter.


Under the reflection of lightning, the rock is colored cyan-blue. Green and yellow lightning flashes arise from the fissures and become entwined with the gravel. The color change in the airwave behind Zenitsu is likewise subtle and precise.

Product Features

  1. From Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba
  2. 21.65 x 19.69 x 20.07 inches (55cm x 50cm x 51cm)
  3. Limited to 1,500 pieces worldwide
  4. Made of polystone, PVC, and resin
  5. Deluxe edition has a swappable portrait with eyes closed and a switchable name plate.
